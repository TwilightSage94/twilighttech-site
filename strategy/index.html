The 45 Minutes

How a session actually runs

Structured but not rigid. We adjust on the fly based on what surfaces — the diagnosis usually drives the rest.

  • Context + current state ~5 min

    Which cert, target date, time budget per week, what you've already tried, what's been hardest. Calibrates everything that follows.

  • Diagnostic pass ~15 min

    Targeted questions across each domain. Not a full practice exam — sampling. The goal is to find the 3-4 sub-objectives that are actually shaky, not the ones you only think you're shaky on.

  • Plan build ~15 min

    We construct your 14-day study path live. You see the reasoning. Adjustments happen in real time so it actually fits your week.

  • Exam-day playbook ~5 min

    Time budget, flag-and-move strategy, PBQ tactics, morning-of routine. The procedural layer most candidates ignore until they're sitting at the testing center.

  • Q&A + send-off ~5 min

    Whatever's still on your mind. You'll get the written plan within 24 hours of the session, so the last 5 minutes is for you, not me.
