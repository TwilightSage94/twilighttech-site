// FAQ

Things people actually ask

What if I'm not sure I need a Build after the Audit?
That's the whole point of the audit being separate. You walk out with a written report you can act on (or not). About a third of audits conclude "you don't need a Build — here's a $50/month tool that solves 80% of this." The audit fee credits toward a Build if you want one, but it's not a contract.
Can I just hire you hourly for an existing problem?
Not at this stage — I'm too small to support hourly engagements without losing the deeper builds. But the Custom tier covers most "I have one specific thing" cases as a fixed-price mini-engagement. Email me with the problem; I'll tell you within 24 hours if it fits.
What if my data can't leave my network?
J-Bot supports a fully air-gapped deployment using local LLMs (Ollama on your hardware). Slower than cloud and capped at smaller models, but zero data leaves your network. Most regulated clients run a hybrid governed by trust gates you control.
How long until I see ROI?
Realistic answer: 4–8 weeks after Build. Week 1–2 your team learns to delegate to it; week 3–4 the agent learns your patterns; by month 2 the savings are measurable. I baseline at the start and re-measure at day 30.
What happens if a model breaks or the API changes?
J-Bot is multi-provider by design — it fails over between NVIDIA NIM, Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, and local Ollama automatically. Provider outages don't take you down. Breaking changes are my problem, not yours.
Who owns the code and configuration after the Build?
You do. The custom domain pack I build for your business is yours — your config, prompts, integrations, data. You can fire me and another engineer can take it over with the source.
What size businesses fit best?
5–50 employees is the sweet spot. 50–200 is workable but integration scope grows. Solo operators often get more value from the local laptop deployment — the audit tells us which path fits.
